Adults

Our aphasia and TBI groups emphasize the nature of compensatory strategies in a safe, fun environment. Patients are encouraged to bring their spouses to participate and engage in activities to strengthen retained skills and build new ones to compensate for effects of the stroke or TBI.

In addition to our groups, we offer individual sessions. These sessions are once a week for 50 minutes. During these sessions, we will target goals that the clinician and the client/caretaker/parent discuss and create together. Please hit the button below to schedule a consultation.

Children

Our autism groups specialize in play-based, PRT sessions that utilize research based practices to elicit language and strengthen communication skills. Our groups allow for practiced social interactions as well as structured activities to work towards attainable goals.

In addition to our groups, we offer individual sessions. These sessions are once a week for 50 minutes. During these sessions, we will target goals that the clinician and the client/caretaker/parent discuss and create together. Please hit the button below to schedule a consultation.

Transgender

Our groups focus on the full communication transformation of transgender voice therapy. Our method includes not only voice but body language, posture, inflections, and feminization. Groups allow participants to practice in a safe environment with peers.

In addition to our groups, we offer individual sessions. These sessions are once a week for 50 minutes. During these sessions, we will target goals that the clinician and the client/caretaker/parent discuss and create together. Please hit the button below to schedule a consultation.
