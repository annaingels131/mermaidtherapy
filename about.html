Meet our Team

Sara Ingels

M.A Speech-Language Pathology, California State University, Fresno

Sara Ingels graduated with her Masters in Speech-Language Pathology in 2016 from California State University, Fresno. Since graduating, she has gained experience working per diem in multiple hospital settings including acute care and outpatient and full-time in the schools. She has experience with autism, childhood language disorders, and phonological/speech sound disorders. She is certificated in Augmentative and Alternative Communicative devices and MTF (Male to Female) Voice Therapy. She is also licensed and ASHA certified. She is currently attending the University of California San Diego for her PhD focusing on literacy and childhood language disorder

Gabriella Castillo

M.A Speech-Language Pathology, California State University, Fresno

Gabriella Castillo graduated with her Masters in Speech-Language Pathology in 2016 from California State University, Fresno. Gabby has honed her skills working in the hospital setting specializing in voice pathologies and dysphagia. In addition, she created and curated Aphasia and Stroke Survivor groups for three years and continues to have a passion for rehabilitation. She is specially trained in fluoroscopy and is an experienced clinician in traumatic brain injury. Her interests also lie with transgender voice therapy as an LGBTQ+ ally and supporter. She is licensed in California and is ASHA certified.
